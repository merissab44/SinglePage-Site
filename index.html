My Photography Journey

I started doing Photography last year around December. I used to only take photos every now and then but since quarantine I really took that up as a serious hobby. I started hiking around my city Oakland,CA and that's when I realized how beautiful my city really was. That's when I became the most interested in landscape. I want to try and get into different editing techniques like black and white, and more city photos.
